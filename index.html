What We Offer

Hospital Services

We provide a wide range of clinical and community health services to patients from across northern Malawi.

🏥

Inpatient Wards

Dedicated wards for medical, surgical, paediatric, and other inpatient care needs.

🩺

Outpatient (OPD)

Consultations, diagnostics, and treatment for patients who do not require overnight admission.

👶

Maternal Health

Antenatal care, skilled delivery services, postnatal follow-up, and family planning.

🌱

Under-5 Clinic

Growth monitoring, vaccinations, and treatment for children under five years of age.
