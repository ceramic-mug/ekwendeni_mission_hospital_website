Our Core Values

What Defines Us

The Christ-centeredness of our hospital is what sets us apart. This is the core belief that drives all that we do.

Christ-Centered

We emulate the values of Christ — love, honesty, equity, compassion, and kindness — in everything we do.

Commitment

We work beyond the call of duty, show consistent dedication to our tasks, and manage our time and resources well.

Integrity

We do the right thing at the right time — whether or not someone is watching — guided by strong moral principles.

Transparency & Accountability

We are open and answerable, taking full responsibility for our actions and the care we provide.

Professionalism

We maintain a positive attitude and strong rapport with staff at all levels and the communities we serve.

What We Offer

Hospital Services

We provide a wide range of clinical and community health services to patients from across northern Malawi.

🏥

Inpatient Wards

250 beds across male, female, maternity, children's, TB, and private wards — specialist nursing care around the clock.
🩺

Outpatient (OPD)

General consultations, chronic care clinics, HIV & AIDS services, mental health, and family planning.
👶

Maternal Health

90-bed maternity ward, neonatal recovery unit, antenatal care, skilled deliveries, and postnatal follow-up.
🌱

Under-5 Clinic

Immunisation, growth monitoring, nutrition assessment, and treatment for children from birth to five years.

2024–2029 Strategic Plan

Pursuing Excellence in Healthcare

In 2024, Ekwendeni Mission Hospital launched its 2024–2029 Strategic Plan with an ambitious goal: to achieve COHSASA (Council for Health Service Accreditation of Southern Africa) accreditation by 30 September 2029.

This milestone reflects our commitment to quality, patient safety, and accountable healthcare for every person we serve — regardless of their ability to pay or how far they have travelled to reach us.

Support Our Work

Facilities at the hospital have steadily improved over the years, thanks to God's blessing and the support of many donor agencies and partner churches. We hope and pray that the hospital is a witness to God, and we aim to serve the surrounding communities to the best of our abilities.
